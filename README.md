# EduConnect101
## Overview:
EduConnect is a portal for students to connect with seniors & teachers & Mentors for their projects, placements & Academic tasks. This promotes peer collobration and motivation among students. 

## Roles:
1. Student
1. Teacher

## Workflow:
1. #### Signup
1. #### Dashboard
1. #### Chat
1. #### Discover
1. #### Friends

1. ### DataBase:
